Our Mission

Making Transit Better

Transit Talk aims to create a transparent record of the issues riders encounter on their commutes, empowering them to make educated decisions about their commutes while also encouraging transit agencies to become more responsive to the needs of their riders.

As a team of volunteers of varying transit-riding backgrounds, we understand the problems commuters face when using public transit. We aim to make everyoneâ€™s commute simpler, and to help all of us make educated choices on each trip.

Transit information should be open and easily accessible. We hope to bridge the gap between transit administrations and their riders, and to allow riders to have more control over their commutes.
